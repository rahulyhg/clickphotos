Clickmania (OPC) Pvt.Ltd. is a registered company under the Indian Companies Act 2013. We are an exclusive site for photographers. We in Clickmania believe a photographer is an artist and not merely a service provider. Our mission is to create a platform for all the photographers irrespective of whether they are professionals or semi-professionals or beginners. We want to give everyone a chance to commercialize their art. The potential clients looking for photographers can search by categories / location / price etc. Whether you are a photographer or looking for a photographer, we have something for you.

Photographers:
  • You can register with us as Free Member or a Silver (paid) / Gold (paid) member for a nominal annual fee.
  • Potential clients can browse through your photographs (only for paid members) and contact you directly. We do not charge any commission.
  • Free members can also be contacted by the clients directly. We do not charge any commission.
  • You can enter as an end user and look for assistant photographers / photography mentors from our list of photographers and contact them directly.
  • Potential client can contact you for high resolution photos and prints for their office / home / place of business.
End users:
  • No registration fee is required.
  • You can search for a photographer of your choice from our list across multiple categories such as weddings, pre-weddings, portfolio, sports, corporate events, family functions etc.
  • You can contact the photographers if you are interested in learning photography as a hobby.
  • You can contact the photographers in our list to buy high resolution photos or prints for your office / home / place of business
